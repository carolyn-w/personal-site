About Me

I'm Carolyn Walker, a front-end developer and technical writer based in rural southern Alberta. I was first introduced to HTML and CSS while studying Technical Communications at Mount Royal University in Calgary, Alberta. I enrolled in the Skillcrush program to further develop my skillset by adding additional courses such as JavaScript, UX/UI, and Python.

When I'm not coding (or learning about coding), I'm traveling, reading, focussing on health and wellness, and lending a hand on my family's cattle ranch.

I look forward to working with you!
